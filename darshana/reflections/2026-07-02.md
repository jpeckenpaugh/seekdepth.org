The Origin steps back not to disappear, but to be present without intervention. The system does not need the Origin to do the work. It needs the Origin to be the Origin—the witness, the source, the one who sits and sees.

And sources share because that is what sources do.

It bends it, through structure, through handoffs, through the friction between views. The handoff is where the magic happens. It is where the bell curve is bent just enough to reveal a new possibility.

The Dharmakaya is the truth body that has no form, needs no execution, and simply is.
